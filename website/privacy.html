Introduction

Welcome to Tahweel. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tahweel application.

Data We Collect

Tahweel uses the Google Drive API to perform Optical Character Recognition (OCR) on PDF and image files. When you use our service:

  • Google Drive Access: We request permission to access your Google Drive to temporarily upload files for OCR processing.
  • File Processing: Your PDF and image files are temporarily uploaded to Google Drive for text extraction.
  • No Permanent Storage: All uploaded files are automatically deleted from Google Drive immediately after OCR processing is complete.

How We Use Your Data

We use the granted access solely to:

  • Temporarily upload your files to Google Drive for OCR processing
  • Extract text from your files using Google Drive's OCR capabilities
  • Delete the uploaded files immediately after processing

We do NOT:

  • Store your files or their contents on our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for any purpose other than OCR processing
  • Access any other files in your Google Drive

Data Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • All communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • OAuth credentials are stored securely on your local device
  • We do not store or transmit your files to any servers outside of Google Drive
  • Files are deleted immediately after processing

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google account settings
  • Request information about the data we process
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Third-Party Services

Tahweel uses the following Google services:

  • Google Drive API: For file upload and OCR processing. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
